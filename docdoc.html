About this app

DocDoc is your personal, secure vault for everything that matters.

From passports to medical records, your important documents stay in one place. Organized. Searchable. Fully under your control.

Built with privacy at its core, DocDoc keeps your data on your device, not on someone else’s server.

🔒 Private by design:

  • Zero-knowledge encryption
  • Data stays on your device
  • No accounts required

📂 Simple organization:

  • Store documents, notes, and files
  • Create folders and categories
  • Add tags and custom fields

🔍 Find anything, instantly:

  • Search by name, category, or details
  • Quickly access what you need, when you need it

📄 Scan and save:

  • Capture documents with built-in scanning
  • Crop, adjust, and store in seconds

🤖 AI, only when you want it:

  • Extract key details from documents
  • Turn files into searchable information
  • Always optional, always in your control

📱 Works everywhere:

  • Install on mobile, tablet, or desktop
  • Works offline, anytime

Whether you're looking to boost productivity or just want a better way to organize your documents, DocDoc has everything you need to succeed. Start your journey today!

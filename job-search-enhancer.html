About this extension

Job hunting is hard. Finding the right job shouldn't be. Cut through the noise and spot language requirements instantly.

Job Search Enhancer fixes the noise so you can focus on the real work: applying to latest jobs. It works quietly in the background to highlight what matters and hide what doesn't.

Colour-coded for clarity (🔴 Required, 🟠 Preferred, 🟢 None).

❓ Why you'll love it:

  • Instant Language Badges - For 7 EU languages
  • Hide the Noise - Remove promoted and reposted jobs
  • Private by Design - No AI, no API keys, no external servers
  • Lightning Fast - Smart caching means results load instantly
  • Accessible UI - Badges meet WCAG AA contrast standards

⛳️ Language Badges for:

  • French
  • Spanish

  • Finnish
